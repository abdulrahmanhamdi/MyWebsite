About This Course

This course introduces the fundamental concepts of continuous-time and discrete-time signals and systems, and provides essential tools for their analysis in both time and frequency domains.

Topics include signal classification, linear time-invariant (LTI) systems, convolution, Fourier series and transforms, Laplace transforms, and Z-transforms. Both theoretical understanding and practical applications in communication and control systems are emphasized.

Students will learn how to model, analyze, and process signals using both analytical and computational methods.
